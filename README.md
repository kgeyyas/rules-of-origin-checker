## 🧭 Overview

**PROVENI** is an open-source Rules of Origin checker designed for importers, exporters, customs professionals, and international trade teams.

It helps determine whether products qualify for preferential tariff treatment under Free Trade Agreements (FTAs) by evaluating origin requirements in a transparent and explainable way.

Incorrect origin determination can lead to:

- Loss of preferential tariff benefits
- Customs disputes
- Compliance violations
- Financial penalties

PROVENI is designed to reduce these risks through structured origin qualification and explainable compliance analysis.

### 🧠 System Design Philosophy

PROVENI is designed around three core principles:

- Explainability over black-box qualification
- Practical compliance over theoretical interpretation
- Transparency in origin decision-making

The goal is not simply determining whether a product qualifies, but helping businesses understand why qualification succeeds or fails and how compliance can be improved.
